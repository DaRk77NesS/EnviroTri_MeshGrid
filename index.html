The Three Pillars of EnviroTri MeshGrid

Three specialized subsystems working in harmony to solve distinct environmental challenges.

PILLAR 01

AWD: Precision Rice Irrigation

AWD-Mesh is a smart irrigation network for rice paddies. It uses water-level sensors, LoRa communication, and weather data to schedule Alternate Wetting and Drying (AWD), cutting methane emissions and saving water while protecting yields.

  • LoRa-connected field and canal sensors
  • Village-wide shared irrigation schedules
  • Reduces water use and methane emissions

PILLAR 02

eCycle Micro-Factory

The eCycle Micro-Factory recovers usable components from e-waste PCBs. AI vision, controlled heating, and gentle robotics depopulate boards, test parts, and feed them back into local repair and IoT projects instead of landfills and smelters.

  • AI identifies chips and components
  • Robotic depopulation and sorting
  • Supplies parts for sensors and controllers
Open eCycle Module
PILLAR 03

HydroLoop: Wastewater to Potable Water

HydroLoop combines Mag-Chito dye removal, smart filtration, and arsenic polishing to turn textile wastewater into safe drinking water. Sensors track color, turbidity, pH, and more to keep every litre accountable.

  • Mag-Chito adsorption for dyes and organics
  • Membrane filtration + UV + light chlorination
  • Arsenic filter for safe, drinkable output
Open Hydro Module

Why These Three Are Connected

Water Crisis: textile factories pollute rivers and over-pump groundwater; communities face arsenic and scarcity.

E-Waste Crisis: discarded electronics from those same regions leak heavy metals and destroy reusable parts.

Agriculture & Climate Crisis: rice paddies emit methane and consume huge volumes of irrigation water.

EnviroTri MeshGrid links them: Treated water from HydroLoop can support communities and farms. Recovered components from eCycle can power the sensors and controllers of AWD and HydroLoop. Smarter irrigation through AWD reduces withdrawals from the same stressed aquifers and canals affected by factories.
